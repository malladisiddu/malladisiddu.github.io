> In this world of illusionary illusions, idignated illusions are riding the world.

A person named Arjun is fond of nothing and everything since his childhood. Which is nothing but he is a jack of all trades but often times master at one when every other person fails. He is not good at studies, that doesn't mean he is bad, he is the above average kind of guy in every thing.
His relatives, neighbours, friends, teachers etc used to ask the questions like Who is your favourite god?(assuming he is an hindu as of now), Whom do you like the most, your Mom or Dad?, What is your favourite color?, What do you want to become when you grow up? etc he answers everything, but actually he doesn't have any genuine answers for any of those questions. Ever since arjun is a child, he is only facinated about math but nothing. He is so intuitive in math, such that he never actually bothered to learn it or practice it. His father used to force him to remember all the 20
*20 tables but he never did. Arjun is very brave but coward at times, very clever but dumb at times, very active but dull at times, very mature but fool at times.
